Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Who We Are

FurEver Care ("we," "us," "our") operates a pet care information website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you visit our Website.

Information We Collect

  • Voluntary Data: Name and email when you subscribe to our newsletter or submit a contact form.
  • Automatic Data: IP address, browser type, OS, device, referring URL, pages visited, time on site — collected via server logs and analytics.
  • Cookies & Tracking: Cookies, pixels, and web beacons for functionality, analytics, and advertising purposes.

How We Use Your Data

  • Operate and improve the Website.
  • Send newsletters you opted into.
  • Analyze traffic with aggregated, anonymized data.
  • Display relevant ads via Google AdSense.
  • Respond to inquiries.
  • Comply with legal requirements.

Google AdSense & Personalized Ads

We use Google AdSense. Google uses cookies (including DoubleClick) for personalized advertising. Opt out at Google Ads Settings or NAI Opt-Out.

Analytics

We may use Google Analytics. Opt out via the GA Opt-out Add-on.

Cookies

  • Essential: Core site function.
  • Analytics: Anonymized visitor data.
  • Advertising: Personalized ad delivery.
  • Functional: Preferences and settings.

Manage cookies via browser settings. Disabling may limit functionality.

Data Sharing

We never sell or rent personal data. Anonymized data may be shared with analytics and ad partners. Third-party services have their own policies.

Data Security

We use HTTPS and industry-standard security measures. No system is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

Not directed at children under 13. We don't knowingly collect their data. Contact us for concerns.
